# Content Video Themes

Taxonomy for the AI-generated video library that powers the swipe-right home feed.
Each theme is a content pillar; prompts are fed directly into Runway/Kling for
generation. All clips are cinematic, abstract/nature/human-subject (no on-camera
text, no logos, no smoking imagery — replacement and forward-motion only), 5–8
seconds, vertical 9:16.

Tags are lowercase, hyphenated, used for search/filtering in `content_videos.tags`.

---

## 1. liberation

The feeling of being untethered — the habit loosening its grip.

- A bird breaking from a flock and rising alone into open sky, golden hour backlight, slow motion
- Hands releasing a paper lantern that drifts upward against a darkening blue sky
- A window thrown open in an old apartment, gauzy curtains billowing outward into bright daylight
- Loose threads unraveling from a tightly wound spool, falling away in slow motion against black
- A rope letting go and snapping free in zero gravity, drifting weightlessly

`tags: freedom, release, sky, motion, breakthrough`

---

## 2. body healing

The body quietly repairing itself, cell by cell, breath by breath.

- Macro shot of new pink skin forming over a healed wound, time-lapse, soft clinical lighting
- Lungs rendered as glowing translucent coral, slowly clearing and brightening with each breath
- A sunrise over misty mountains synced to a slow inhale-exhale rhythm, color warming gradually
- Blood cells flowing smoothly through a vein, abstract bioluminescent visualization, calm blue tones
- A wilted houseplant slowly uncurling and turning green again in time-lapse sunlight

`tags: healing, lungs, breath, recovery, body`

---

## 3. clarity of mind

Mental fog lifting; sharper focus and quiet presence.

- Fog rolling back off a still lake at dawn, revealing crisp reflections of pine trees
- A camera lens slowly racking into perfect focus on a single dew drop on a leaf
- Ripples on dark water settling into a perfectly still, mirror-flat surface
- Static on an old TV screen resolving into a clear, vivid mountain landscape
- A cluttered desk shown in fast-motion being cleared down to one clean, sunlit notebook

`tags: focus, calm, clarity, stillness, mind`

---

## 4. milestone celebration

Marking progress — the quiet pride of another day, week, month won.

- Confetti of dry autumn leaves swirling upward in slow motion against warm sunset light
- A single candle flame growing brighter and taller on a minimalist cake, soft bokeh background
- Footprints in fresh snow trailing off toward a bright horizon, each step deliberate
- A hand drawing a checkmark in chalk on dark slate, dust scattering in slow motion
- Fireworks blooming silently and slowly against a deep navy sky, reflected in calm water

`tags: milestone, progress, celebration, streak, achievement`

---

## 5. nature and breath

Grounding in the natural world; breath as anchor and rhythm.

- Tall grass rippling in waves across a sunlit field, wind sound implied through motion
- Ocean waves breathing in and out against a quiet shoreline at dusk, slow tide
- Sunlight filtering through a dense forest canopy, dust motes drifting in golden shafts
- A single tree's branches swaying gently against a clear blue sky, time-lapse clouds behind
- Steam rising slowly from a hot spring into cold mountain air at sunrise

`tags: nature, breathing, grounding, outdoors, slow`

---

## 6. social reconnection

Showing up fully for the people and moments that matter.

- Two silhouettes' hands reaching toward each other across a sunlit kitchen table, slow motion
- A family's shadows playing together on a sunny lawn, laughter implied through motion blur
- Friends' silhouettes clinking glasses at golden hour, warm bokeh city lights behind
- A child's hand pulling an adult's hand toward a playground, joyful forward motion
- A dinner table filling with warm light and gentle motion as more chairs are pulled in

`tags: connection, family, friends, presence, relationships`

---

## 7. morning ritual

The first hour of the day, reclaimed and reshaped.

- Steam curling off a fresh cup of coffee or tea on a sunlit windowsill, slow motion
- Bare feet stepping onto cool morning grass, dew catching the first light
- Curtains slowly brightening a bedroom as the sun rises outside a window
- A journal page filling with handwriting in time-lapse, soft desk lamp light
- Running shoes being laced up on a porch step as fog lifts over a quiet street

`tags: morning, ritual, routine, fresh-start, habits`

---

## 8. craving as a wave

Visualizing the urge itself — building, cresting, and passing, to reinforce that it always does.

- A single ocean wave rising, cresting, and dissolving smoothly back into calm water
- Storm clouds gathering, darkening, then parting to reveal clear sky, sped up
- A wave of sand dunes shifting and settling under wind, slow motion, desert gold light
- Turbulent river rapids gradually widening and slowing into a calm, glassy pool
- A pulse of light traveling through fiber-optic strands, intensifying then fading to soft glow

`tags: urge-surfing, wave, impermanence, regulation, coping`

---

## 9. small wins

Everyday, low-stakes proof that the new pattern is sticking.

- A glass jar slowly filling with coins or marbles, one dropping in at a time, soft light
- A simple wall calendar with each day's box filling in with a small hand-drawn mark
- A plant on a windowsill growing taller across a subtle time-lapse, leaves unfurling
- Water filling an empty glass measuring cup line by line, steady and deliberate
- A row of dominoes set upright one by one, ending mid-set without falling — control, not chaos

`tags: small-wins, consistency, habit-building, proof, momentum`

---

## 10. quiet strength

Resolve without grit-teeth intensity — calm, grounded resilience.

- A lighthouse standing steady against slow-rolling waves at dusk, beam sweeping calmly
- A single oak tree standing firm as wind moves visibly through a field around it
- Hands steadily holding a small flame cupped against a light breeze, flame staying lit
- A mountain peak emerging slowly above a sea of moving clouds at sunrise
- Tai chi-like silhouette moving slowly and deliberately against a soft gradient sky

`tags: resilience, strength, calm, steadiness, resolve`

---

## Generation notes

- No on-screen text, logos, brand marks, or smoking/vaping paraphernalia of any kind.
- No people's faces in close-up unless fully silhouetted or backlit (keeps content universal, avoids uncanny-valley AI faces).
- Favor slow motion and steady camera moves — the feed should feel calming to swipe through, never frenetic.
- Each prompt should be self-contained and renderable as a single 5–8 second clip with no narrative dependency on prior clips.
